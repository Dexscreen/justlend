Frequently Asked Questions

What is JustLend?

JustLend DAO is the leading decentralized lending protocol on TRON. Supply crypto to earn APY, borrow against collateral, stake TRX, and govern via JST token at justlend-app.org.

How do I start using JustLend?

Connect your TronLink or WalletConnect wallet to justlend-app.org. Supply assets, borrow against collateral, stake TRX, or swap TRC20 tokens from the JustLend dashboard.

What is the JST token?

JST is the governance token of JustLend DAO. JST holders vote on protocol proposals and earn a share of JustLend protocol revenues through staking.

What collateral is required to borrow?

JustLend requires overcollateralized loans — typically 125–200% depending on the asset. Collateral factors are set by JustLend DAO governance and visible in the live markets dashboard.

Is JustLend safe to use?

JustLend's smart contracts are independently audited. Decentralized oracles, algorithmic risk controls, and DAO governance maintain security. DeFi always carries smart contract risk.

What is JustLend Energy Rental?

JustLend Energy Rental lets you rent TRON network Energy to reduce transaction fees by up to 80%. Ideal for high-frequency DeFi users who want to minimize costs on TRON.
